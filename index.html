ꜛAbout

Hi! I am a 3rd year phd student at Université Paris-Saclay in the Ex-Situ team, under the supervision of Theophanis Tsandilas and Cédric Fleury. I study collaborative 3D modelling using Augmented Reality (AR).

I am a former research engineer from the LSI team at CEA LIST. I took part in various projects, from motion capture for character animation in Virtual Reality (VR) to tangible interactions for collaborative AR.

I am interested in developing new interactions with AR and VR, as I believe these technologies might help ideation and facilitate collaboration.
