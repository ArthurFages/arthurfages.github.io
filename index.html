Hi! I am a HCI post-doctoral fellow in the ILDA team of the Laboratoire Interdisciplinaire des Sciences du Numérique (LISN) on the InterPlay research project.

I did a PhD at Université Paris-Saclay in the Ex-Situ team, under the supervision of Theophanis Tsandilas and Cédric Fleury, and defended my thesis on "Collaborative Design in Augmented Reality (AR) : Supporting multiple views and parallel versions of the design space" in April 2023.

I am a former research engineer from the LSI team at CEA LIST. I took part in various projects, from motion capture for character animation in Virtual Reality (VR) to tangible interactions for collaborative AR.

While questionning the current and likely use of AR and VR, I am interested in developing new interactions techniques with these technologies, as I believe they might help ideation and facilitate collaboration.
